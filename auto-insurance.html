Auto Insurance Coverage Calculator

Not all auto insurance coverage is the same. Many people overpay for coverages they don’t need, while others don’t have enough coverage but don’t know until it’s too late. This tool will help you decide what coverage options you need. It will make shopping around for the best insurance rates a lot easier, and it takes less than one minute!

Do you own your vehicle?
  • Leased vehicles automatically need high liability coverage, collision coverage and comprehensive coverage.

Is your vehicle less than 1 year old?
Have you paid more than 20% of the vehicle’s price?

If you have paid only a small portion down on your vehicle, it is wise to buy gap insurance to protect you if it is totaled.

Do you and your frequent passengers have health insurance?

If you’re injured by an uninsured driver, your medical bills would not be covered unless you have health insurance or have purchased uninsured motorist bodily injury, PIP or medical payments coverage.

Could you afford to replace your vehicle without the help of insurance?

If you can afford to replace your vehicle, and you want the lowest rates possible, you should not buy collision or comprehensive coverage.

How much money do you have easy access to if your vehicle is damaged?

If there is damage to your vehicle that you can afford to fix yourself, you may want to pay out of pocket instead of filing a claim, because claims increase your rates in the long run. This will help determine your deductible.

Do you want the lowest possible rates or the best protection?

Some consumers don't mind paying more for auto insurance to feel more secure that they'll be covered for certain incidents. Others want the lowest rates possible, even if it means less coverage. If you choose "best protection," we will only suggest you buy insurance coverage that makes sense given your other answers. We will not suggest you buy more coverage than you need. Likewise, if you choose "lowest rates," we will consider your other answers and suggest what makes sense. We will not suggest you buy less coverage than you absolutely need.

Choosing the right coverage

Getting the right level of auto insurance is not one size fits all. What is most important to one consumer isn’t necessary for another.

That’s why it’s critical to get the auto insurance that fits your needs.

This Auto Insurance Coverage Calculator is a quick way to find the right auto insurance levels and options for you. While getting the cheapest auto insurance is most important for some consumers, another person may want the broadest possible protection. Our calculator understands that personalization is vital when deciding on the best auto insurance coverage for your situation.
