Non-QM Loan Programs

For Highly Qualified Self-Employed Borrowers

Qualify with 12-months of bank statements and get a floor rate as low as 4.99%

  • Loans up to $3M | Cash out refi to $2.5M
  • Debt to income ratio up to 43% allowed with minimum FICO score of 680
  • Minimum reserves of 6 months and no mortgage lates for 24 months is required
  • One Borrower must be self-employed and may have multiple streams of income

For Self-Employed Borrowers

Qualify with 12-months bank statements

  • Loans up to $3M | Cash out refi to $2.5M | DTI up to 55%
  • Min FICO score 600 | Min reserves - 3 months
  • No more than 1 mortgage late in past 12 months
  • One borrower must be self-employed and may have multiple streams of income

For Professional Real Estate Investors

Qualify with subject property cash flow

  • Loans up to $2.5M | Cash out refi to $2.0M | DCR = 1.0
  • Unlimited financed properties allowed
  • Borrower does not need to own a primary residence or investment property
  • No tax returns, no employment, no income and no pre-payment penalty

For Borrowers with High Cash Liquidity

Qualify based on vierified liquid assets

  • Loan amounts up to $3M | Cash out refi up to $2.5M
  • Usable Assets: 100% Checking & Savings, 90% Stocks, Bonds & Mutual Funds, 80% Retirement Funds
  • No DTI ratio calculated | Min FICO score 600 | No employment or income on 1003

For Conforming & Jumbo Loans, and Owners of Multiple Properties Falling Just Outside Qualified Mortgage Parameters

Qualify with full documentation

  • Loan amounts up to $3M | Cash out refi up to $2.5M | DTI up to 55%
  • Cash out up to 85% LTV (max. $1.0M)
  • Min FICO score 600 | Min reserves - 3 months
  • Up to 8 financed properties allowed
