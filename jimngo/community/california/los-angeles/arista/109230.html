Community Details of Arista

Welcoming porched entries. Great rooms that merge cooking, dining mingling, and fresh-air spaces. Options to option your heart out. These new homes were designed to flex with you, to this ever-evolving, uber-demanding thing called life. Choose from one and two-story plans in architectural styles true to the area’s roots, including contemporary Spanish, modern adobe, and modern farmhouse. Personalize with a den or another bedroom. Opt for a fifth bedroom in place of the 3rd bay in the garage. In the event of a teenager, there’s a room perfectly suited for hours of selfies and gossip, aptly named the “Teen Room.” Expand your living space and your views of Santa Clarita’s wondrous wildscape with optional outdoor room, a breezy space designed to connect indoors and out seamlessly. Let’s just say, it’s easy to find your sweet spot in Arista. Arista—located in the master-planned community of Aliento, an exclusive, gated enclave in Santa Clarita—sits among terraced hillsides and ridgelines, and is walking distance to the acclaimed elementary school and miles of trails that connect to vistas of city lights and the perks of nearby shopping and dining. Central clubhouse with pools, splash pad, and fitness center coming soon.
