Community Details of Travata - Palencia

Let us find your perfect home tailored to fit your needs. 949-234-7880 | LENNARiTeam@Lennar.com You'll be drawn to the allure of this resort-style 55+ community, alive with bustling activities and tranquil spaces. Plus, No Mello Roos! Discover an unforgettable collection of spacious homes for sale at Palencia, one of the three neighborhoods in the upscale Travata masterplan exclusively for active adults. Palencia offers two-story floorplan designs that showcase details such as open-concept living areas, gourmet kitchens with center islands, private backyards, and expansive master suites.
